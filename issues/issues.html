Issues We Face

The reason that we don’t believe in gun control laws is primarily that they seem to be ineffective, regardless of how strict states make them. It should be made known that if citizens gave up their right to bear arms, the government would have the opportunity to turn against the citizens by taking away all guns or causing government tyranny. Another reason we don’t believe in gun control is that we found that it’s not the gun control laws that deter crime, it’s the gun ownership that deters crime. We found that gun control laws can’t, don’t, and won't do anything at all. In fact, going to our point before, gun ownership had dropped violent crime rates much more dramatically than gun control laws.

We see that current gun control laws are targeted at poor, black communities because these laws were founded on racial fears. The Statera Party is strongly against discrimination of any kind. We believe that taking away some guns is an infringement of the second amendment and self-defense, as well as denying a sense of safety to U.S. citizens. It would be unnecessary to impose gun control laws when they only make things worse in terms of freedom and safety. The Statera Party is opposing gun control because it conflicts with the freedom to bear arms and self-defense.
